UF

  • Amrut Filtration offers cost-effective and high-quality systems for residential, commercial, and industrial use.
  • UF is a crucial purification technology that effectively removes a wide range of contaminants, including bacteria, viruses, proteins, and plastics, making it a key component for producing high-purity water.
  • This process serves as a pre-treatment step for other systems like RO and ion exchange, boasting benefits such as being eco-friendly, requiring no chemicals, and featuring a modular design for easy setup and maintenance.
  • Our plants come with a high output rate, advanced monitoring, and sturdy frames to ensure reliable and efficient performance across various applications, from packaged drinking water to wastewater recycling.

DM

  • The demineralisation process, using Ion Exchange Resins, removes all unwanted minerals from water.
  • First, water passes through a cation resin column to remove positively charged ions (cations). Then, it goes through an anion resin column to remove negatively charged ions (anions). This two-step process, perfected by experts at Amrut Filtration, ensures the water is pure and safe to drink..
  • As a leading DM plant manufacturer, Amrut Filtration exports its perfectly engineered plants to numerous countries, including Kenya, Ethiopia, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Burundi, Comoros, and many others in Africa and beyond.
